A few words about us

Dr Ramona Raggl is a Clinical Neuropsychologist who has specialist training and experience working with children. Ramona completed her undergraduate training at the University of Southern Queensland (in 1999) and a Doctor of Psychology (Clinical Neuropsychology) post-graduate degree at Latrobe University (Melbourne) (in 2003). She is a full member of the Australian Psychology Society and the College of Clinical Neuropsychologists.

During her Doctoral training Ramona completed a thesis that produced a series of insightful studies examining the assessment and remediation of organisational difficulties that are inherent in children with Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder. She also gained valuable clinical experience through her work in various Melbourne hospitals including – Sunshine Hospital, Caulfield General Medical Centre, Darebin Community Mental Health Centre, Northern Hospital, and a twelve month internship at the Royal Children’s Hospital.

Ramona was employed at The Queensland Paediatric Rehabilitation Service - Royal Children’s Hospital (Brisbane) - from 2004 to 2009, and commenced her Private Practice (PDLC) in 2007. She has gone on to present a number of research posters at various national and international conferences on paediatric topics.

Our Services

Diagnostic Evaluation:

  • Neuropsychology assessment accompanied by a clinical interview
  • Assessment of cognitive/thinking skills
  • Academic assessment (e.g. literacy and numeracy)
  • Assessment of behaviour, emotional functioning and social skills

Treatment and Follow-up Support:

  • Comprehensive report summarising results
  • Verbal feedback of results to parents and teachers
  • Recommendation of individualised classroom strategies customised to each child’s needs.
